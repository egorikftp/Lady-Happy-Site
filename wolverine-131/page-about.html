ABOUT US

Wolverine Creative is an award winning digital media, web design and PR agency. In 2012, Wolverine Creative was recognized as one of the top 3 Advertising Agencies in Wisconsin by readers of Corporate Report Wisconsin. The company recently received two gold Hermes Awards for design and five Alchemy Awards from the Public Relations Society of America, more than any other agency in Madison, Wisconsin.

Wolverine Creative was also a 2006 Wisconsin Better Business Bureau Business Ethics Torch award finalist and has been recognized twice by Madison Magazine as one of the best places to work in Dane County.
